# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We are committed to making participation in the IBeam community a harassment-free and constructive experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and professional in all interactions.
- Assume good intent and communicate with clarity.
- Give feedback on code and ideas, not people.
- Help create an inclusive environment for contributors of all experience levels.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, threats, or intimidation.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, and community spaces where project representation is implied.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may take action they deem appropriate for violations, including warning, temporary restriction, or removal from community channels.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the maintainers.
